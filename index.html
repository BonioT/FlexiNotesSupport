Frequently Asked Questions

How do I change the page?

It's simple: just swipe left or right on the screen. It works exactly like flipping the pages of a real notebook.

Can I export my notes?

Yes! Tap the share icon in the top right corner to instantly export your current page as a high-quality image.

Are my notes private?

Absolutely. FlexiNotes saves your drawings and layouts exclusively on your local device. We have zero access to your data.

Does it work offline?

100%. You don't need any internet connection to write, draw, or browse through your past notes.
