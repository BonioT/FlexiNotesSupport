Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 18, 2026

Protecting your privacy is our top priority. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in relation to your use of the FlexiNotes mobile application.

1. Data Collection

The FlexiNotes app is designed with a privacy-first approach. Use our digital planner with complete peace of mind, knowing your ideas and sketches are entirely safe.

No Personal Data: We do not collect, store, or share any personal information such as your name, email address, or phone number.

No Tracking: We do not track your location, your activity within the app, or any usage metrics.

No Third-Party Services: We do not use third-party SDKs, analytics tools, or advertising networks that collect or transmit user data.

2. Local Storage

All your custom grids, drawings, and daily pages are stored exclusively on your device. This data is never transmitted to any of our servers or external databases.

3. Children's Privacy

Since we do not collect any personal data, FlexiNotes is completely safe for users of all ages, including children.

4. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
